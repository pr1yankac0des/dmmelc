Our Products

Proprietary AI frameworks built for enterprise-scale performance and security.

EDIS

EDIS is an AI-powered Business Intelligence (BI) chatbot platform that extracts strategic and operational insights from unstructured, multimodal business content—including PDFs, PowerPoint presentations, SharePoint files, and Tableau dashboards. It empowers CXOs with real-time answers without waiting on data teams.

Learn More

MelC

MelC is an AI-powered Conversational Audit Tool offering a multilingual audio transcription and audit platform. It enhances customer satisfaction by ensuring accurate communication. Designed to streamline the auditing process, it makes analyzing customer interactions and deriving actionable insights easier and faster.

Visit Website

BoQx

BoQx is an AI-powered image extraction tool that transforms complex CAD drawings into precise Bills of Quantities (BOQs) instantly. Designed for architects and engineers, it automates manual quantity estimation—delivering speed, accuracy, and consistency across projects of any size.
